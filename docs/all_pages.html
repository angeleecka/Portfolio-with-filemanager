OUR TEAM

Visagiste

The ability to see and create unique images, from natural makeup to complex makeup. The ability to work with facial features, emphasizing advantages and hiding disadvantages. Expertise in selecting cosmetics suitable for different skin types and shooting conditions.

Stylist

Deep knowledge of hairstyles of different eras. Mastery of various styling and hair working techniques, which allows you to embody any, even the most complex historical image. The ability not only to recreate, but also to adapt a historical hairstyle to a modern type, so that the image looks harmonious and convincing.

Cameraman

Ability to work with light, composition and angles to create atmospheric and expressive images. Deep knowledge of photo and video equipment to achieve the highest quality of shooting. Ability to direct the model to achieve the desired emotion and story in the frame.

Dresser

Ability to recreate costumes from different eras with attention to the smallest details. Skills in working with different fabrics and materials, from sewing to decorating. Ability to create not only beautiful, but also comfortable costumes that will withstand all filming conditions.
